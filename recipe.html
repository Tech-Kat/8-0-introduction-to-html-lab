The Best Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs is a quick and easy appetizer for your family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 to 2 dashes Tabasco sauce, to taste
  • Salt, to taste
  • Â¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon snipped fresh chives
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • Paprika, for garnish
  • Whole fresh chives, for garnish

Directions

  1. Rinse eggs with warm water, and place in a small saucepan. Cover with cold water, place the pan over medium-high heat and bring to a boil.
  2. Turn off heat, cover and let sit for 10-12 minutes.
  3. Drain, rinse under cold water and peel. Cool in the refrigerator, loosely covered, for 15 minutes.
  4. Halve eggs lengthwise, and carefully scoop out yolks. Place yolks in a bowl, and mash with a fork.
  5. Add mustard, Tabasco, salt, pepper and snipped chives. Stir in mayonnaise.
  6. Fill each egg white with about 1 1/2 teaspoons of the egg-yolk mixture and dust the top with paprika.
  7. Arrange in a spoke design on a platter; garnish with whole chives.
